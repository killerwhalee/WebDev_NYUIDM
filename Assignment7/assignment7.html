Introduction!

Marshall McLuhan introduced the groundbreaking concept that the medium through which information is conveyed is as significant as the content itself.

Medium and Message Interaction

The interaction between the medium and the message is a central theme, emphasizing how the medium profoundly influences the interpretation of content.

Influence on Perception

The characteristics of a medium play a crucial role in shaping the way individuals perceive and interpret the information it conveys.

Media's Role in Societal Change

McLuhan's theory extends to societal levels, highlighting how changes in media technology have the power to shape cultural norms, values, and communication patterns.

Ubiquity of Media in Daily Life

The omnipresence of media in daily life is considered, emphasizing how this constant exposure has far-reaching implications for individuals and societies.

My Opinion!

From text on paper to the web, I really feel the speed of media is getting faster and faster. We may think this improves collecting data from others, but sometimes I think we need to slow down since our brains and consciousness are not as fast as the media. This could lead to our brains burning out.
